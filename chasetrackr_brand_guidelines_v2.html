Iconography Style
Use SF Symbols (iOS) at Regular weight — clean, rounded, approachable. Color icons with Electric Blue (#2979FF) for primary actions, Cyan (#00D4FF) for highlights, and Gold (#F5A623) for chase/rarity indicators. Avoid overly detailed or heavy stroke icons.

Logo Usage Rules
✓
Correct Use
Blue gradient or solid blue backgrounds. Navy dark mode. White surfaces with blue wordmark.
✗
No Stretching
Never distort, skew, or alter proportions of the wordmark for any reason.
✓
Approved Tints
Full color, all-white (on dark), and navy+blue (on light) are the only approved colorways.
✗
No Recoloring
Never replace the cyan accent on "Trackr" with another color or gradient.
✓
Minimum Size
Never smaller than 80px wide on screen or 0.8in in print. Test legibility of "Trackr".
✗
No Outlines/Shadows
Don't add strokes, drop shadows, or emboss effects to the logotype text itself.
Voice & Tone
✓ Do
"Luffy OP05 TR — up 18% this week. Fair value: $240"
✗ Don't
"The estimated current market price for this card is approximately $240.00 USD."
✓ Do
"Chase alert — price dropped below your target. Buy window is open."
✗ Don't
"Please be advised that a price change event has occurred on this card."
